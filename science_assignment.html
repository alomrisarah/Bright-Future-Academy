Science Assignment: Physics Experiment Report

Assignment Overview

This assignment requires you to conduct a physics experiment, analyze the results, and write a detailed report. The experiment can be based on topics from your class, such as Newton's Laws or the laws of thermodynamics. Ensure your report includes clear diagrams, calculations, and references to sources of information used in your research.

Details

  • Due Date: December 2, 2024
  • Format: PDF or Word document with diagrams
  • Report Length: Minimum 1000 words
  • References: At least 2 peer-reviewed articles or textbooks

Submission Instructions

Submit the completed report by uploading it via the student portal by the due date. Be sure to include clear charts or images illustrating your experiment results. Late submissions will be penalized by 5% for each day after the due date.
