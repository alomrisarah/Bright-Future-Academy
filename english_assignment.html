English Assignment: Essay on Climate Change

Assignment Overview

This assignment requires you to write an essay on the topic of "Climate Change: Causes, Effects, and Solutions." The essay should be well-researched, with references to scientific articles and reliable sources.

Details

  • Due Date: November 30, 2024
  • Word Count: 1500-2000 words
  • Format: Microsoft Word or PDF
  • References: At least 3 credible sources (books, journals, websites)

Submission Instructions

Submit your completed essay by uploading it via the student portal before the due date. Late submissions will incur a penalty of 10% per day.
