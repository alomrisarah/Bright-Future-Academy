History Assignment: World War II Timeline

Assignment Overview

Your task is to create a detailed timeline of significant events that occurred during World War II. This includes military battles, political shifts, treaties, and key events such as D-Day and the signing of the Treaty of Versailles. You must research the events, present them chronologically, and include brief descriptions of each event.

Details

  • Due Date: December 5, 2024
  • Format: Interactive timeline (can be digital or on poster board)
  • Length: Minimum 10 events with explanations
  • References: Use at least 3 historical sources

Submission Instructions

Submit your timeline by uploading a photo of your timeline to the student portal by the due date. Ensure that all events are clearly marked and described, and that the timeline is visually appealing. If creating a digital timeline, you may use any free online timeline tool or presentation software.
